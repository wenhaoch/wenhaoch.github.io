Model Inputs


The following inputs were used in the final Neural Network architecture that our team has developed:

  • Mean dengue cases of periods T-12 to T-9, T-8 to T-5, and T-4 to T-1
  • Mean first-order differential of dengue levels of periods T-12 to T-9 and T-4 to T-1
  • Mean second-order differential of dengue levels from period T-4 to T-1
  • Population levels at period T+0
  • Maximum and mean values of rainfall from period T-3 to T+0 (Rainfall clusters 2, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11)
  • Minimum and mean values of temperature from periods T-12 to T-8 and T-7 to T-4 (Temperature clusters 2, 3, 4)
