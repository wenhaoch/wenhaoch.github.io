Co-Curricular Activities and Community Involvement

Workshop Director and Facilitator

2016 - 2018

Indochina Starfish Foundation (Phnom Penh, Cambodia)

Organised and facilitated twice-yearly youth empowerment workshops for the youths under the Employment Programme of the NGO. Incorporating activities imparting soft skills such as critical thinking, resilience building, and self-reflection, this series of workshops aim to get the youths kickstarted in making a change in their own local communities as they learn how to start their own community development projects and fund them through various fundraising activities.


Project Director, tChamps

2017 - 2018

Led an interest group in Tembusu College aimed at responsible overseas community development, liaising with Indochina Starfish Foundation (ISF) to plan and organise empowerment workshops for teenagers in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.

Workshops aside, I've spearheaded fundraising activities as well as student sharings to educate fellow undergraduates in the college on the community involvement scene in Cambodia, and how a culture of responsible volunteering can be nutured.
